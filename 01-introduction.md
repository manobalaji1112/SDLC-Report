# Introduction to SDLC

## What is SDLC?

SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) is a structured process used to develop high-quality software. It defines the stages involved in software development from planning to maintenance.

## Why SDLC is Important?

* Improves software quality
* Reduces project risks
* Helps complete projects on time
* Ensures customer requirements are met
* Makes development organized and systematic
